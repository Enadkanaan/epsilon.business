About Epsilon Advisory

A senior-led advisory firm for organizations preparing for their next stage of growth

Epsilon Advisory supports organizations that have outgrown informal ways of working and need stronger clarity, structure, governance, and execution discipline.

We help leadership teams make sense of where they are, define where they need to go, and build the operating foundations required to get there.

Our advisory model combines two complementary disciplines: innovation management and corporate readiness, alongside organization design and development. This allows us to support clients from strategic ambition through to the roles, processes, governance systems, and routines needed for delivery.

We do not deliver generic playbooks. We design practical advisory solutions that reflect each organization's maturity, ambition, market reality, and internal capacity.

What We Do

Advisory services designed around real organizational challenges

Our services are packaged into clear advisory products. Each product is designed to solve a specific leadership, growth, governance, innovation, or operating model challenge.

Strategy & Growth Readiness

For organizations that need strategic clarity, leadership alignment, and a practical route to execution.

We help leadership teams reset direction, agree on priorities, and translate ambition into a 12-month execution roadmap. Engagements include the Strategy Reset Sprint, Annual Operating Plan Lab, and Executive Strategy Clinic — each delivering clear strategic priorities, initiative portfolios, and KPI logic that teams can act on immediately.

Organization Design & Operating Model

For organizations whose structure, roles, decision-making, or accountability have not kept up with growth.

We design the target operating model, clarify functional mandates, establish decision rights, and build RACI frameworks and KPI systems. Engagements include Operating Model Design, Organization Restructuring Package, and Performance & Accountability System — leaving clients with a structure built for their next stage.

Innovation & Corporate Readiness

For organizations that want to innovate, launch new initiatives, or collaborate with startups in a structured way.

We assess innovation maturity, design governance models, and build use-case frameworks and startup engagement processes. Engagements include the Innovation Readiness Assessment, Corporate Innovation Operating System, and Startup Collaboration Readiness Program — giving organizations the infrastructure to manage innovation effectively.

Governance & Institutional Systems

For organizations that need stronger documentation, policies, SOPs, committees, and management controls.

We establish governance models, delegation of authority frameworks, SOP libraries, committee charters, and management reporting templates. Engagements include Governance & SOP Setup, Board & Committee Effectiveness, and Founder-to-Management Transition — building the institutional backbone for sustainable growth.
